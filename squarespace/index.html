How to get started with Member Areas

Get Started

  1. Create a website with Squarespace, and decide on a name for your member area.

  2. Add members-only content, from classes to newsletters, guides to videos.

  3. Customize your member area landing and sign-up page with details about what you offer.

  4. Connect your preferred processor so you can start receiving fees.

  5. Add a Member Areas subscription to your existing Squarespace website plan.
